Troubleshooting your Plantr device

Try these steps to connect your app to your Plantr device:

  1. Refresh the app by double-tapping the home button, swiping up on the Plantr app window, and re-opening it.
  2. Make sure you have opened the lights tab before trying to connect to the Plantr device.
  3. Make sure bluetooth and new bluetooth connections are on in the settings.
  4. Restart your mobile device by turning it on and off again by holding down the power button, sliding the power off slider, and then one powered off, holding the power button again.
  5. Unplug and replug your Plantr device. Allow up to 60 seconds for the device to start up.
